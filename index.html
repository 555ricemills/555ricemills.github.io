Sachit Agarwal

Sachit Agarwal had a humble beginning. Working in a private firm, he always had a big dream. Eventually he quit his job and started 555 Rice Mills in Bijaynagar. Sachit's wife, Swati Agarwal played a major role in encouraging and motivating him in the early days when the mill only produced 20 units per day, but now we have become the market leaders, providing the best quality of rice at minimal cost. Honest and hardworking, his honesty has given birth to the tagline Parampara Bishwas Ki.

Alok Gupta

Alok Gupta has been associated with Sachit Agarwal since the inception of 555 Rice Mills. He has handled the distribution while Sachit Agarwal handled the production and quality control. Together we aim to provide you with the best quality of rice at minimum cost in North East India.
